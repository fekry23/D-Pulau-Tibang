    • A N G G E R I K

    There are two types of rooms. Anggerik Chalet is suitable for 2 people, meanwhile Anggerik dorm is suitable for 8 people.

    M A W A R

    There are two types of rooms. Mawar Single Bed room suitable for 2 people, meanwhile Mawar Double Decker is suitable for 4 people.

    C E M P A K A

    Embrace the old fashioned values by staying at Cempaka Chalet. Suitable for 2-3 people per room. 10 unit only.

    H O M E S T A Y

    Homestay suitable for family who is on a vacation. The homestay comes with a bathroom and kitchen. 2 unit only.

    C A M P S I T E

    Feel the calmness of nature by renting/staying at the camp base provided. Tents are availble for rent, or can bring your own tent.

    O R K I D

    Two types of room, the first one is called Orkid Studio, meanwhile the second one is called Orkid Dorm. Orkid Dorm is an accommodation suitable for people of large groups who is looking for a place to stay at D' Pulau Tibang.
