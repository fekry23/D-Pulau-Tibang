    To make a reservation, please email the following information to dtibangcamp@yahoo.com or contact us throught Whatsapp:

     

    1. Reservation requirements:
       Full name
      MyKad/ Passport Number
      Contact Number
      Total number of guests & their full names (children included)
       
    2. Emergency contact:
      Full name
      Contact number / Email
       
    3. Choice of chalet(s) from the following list:
       Anggerik
      Mawar
      Cempaka
      Homestay
      Campsite
      Orkid
       
    4. Check in date and check out date
        

    Full payment for your stay is required at check-in or paid via bank transfer within 24 hours to confirm your reservation.

    After a payment is made via online, please contact through Whatsapp or email us the receipt and we will confirm your reservation.

    We deploy all efforts to protect the private nature of your personal information. We do not share your information with any third parties, unless we are required or authorised to do so by law.

    Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we cannot accept entry of guests who are having any of the following symptoms:

    • Fever (above 38°C(100.4°F))
    • Dry cough
    • Sore throat
    • Difficulty Breathing
    • Flu

     

    If guests are experiencing any of the above mentioned symptoms after a confirmed booking and payments made, inform us as soon as possible. We can work on an alternative available date.

     

    CANCELLATION

    Please inform us via email or Whatsapp at least 48 hours before your check in date to cancel your reservation with a full refund. Cancellations made within less than the 48 hours are not eligible for any refunds.

    If you would like to change your reservation to a different date (depending on availability), please inform us via email or Whatsapp at least 48 hours before your check in date.​
